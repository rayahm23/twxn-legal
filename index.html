Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create a Twxn account, we collect:

  • Email address — provided directly or via Sign in with Apple / Google
  • Display name and username — set during onboarding or profile setup
  • Profile bio — optional text you write yourself
  • Profile photo — uploaded from your photo library (optional)

We do not collect your real name unless you choose to use it as your display name.

1.2 Beauty & Taste Profile

To power our recommendations, we collect information you provide during onboarding and profile setup:

  • Product categories you like and dislike (e.g. skincare, haircare, fragrance)
  • Product tags and aesthetics (e.g. "clean beauty," "dewy," "fragrance-free")
  • Brands you like or dislike
  • Price range preferences
  • Skin type and skin tone
  • Hair type, porosity, processing history, and wash frequency
  • Scalp type, climate, age bracket, and spend tier
  • Taste archetype (computed from your preferences)

This information is used exclusively to personalise your product recommendations and is never used for advertising or sold to third parties.

1.3 Activity & Engagement

  • Swipe history — products you liked or passed on (product ID + timestamp)
  • Ratings — star ratings, traits, and optional written reviews
  • Saved, liked, and bought product lists
  • Follow relationships — accounts you follow on Twxn

1.4 Chat Messages (Rexy AI)

When you chat with Rexy, our AI shopping advisor:

  • Your messages and conversation history are sent to our servers and forwarded to Groq Inc. (our AI provider) to generate a response.
  • Your taste profile (categories, tags, brands, skin type, price range) is included in each request so Rexy can give personalised advice.
  • Conversation history is stored locally on your device and in your Twxn account.
  • Groq may temporarily process this data for safety monitoring. Refer to Groq's Privacy Policy for their independent data practices.
Important: Do not share sensitive personal information (medical details, financial data, passwords) in chat. Rexy is designed for beauty product discovery only.

1.5 Contacts (Optional)

If you choose to use "Find Friends from Contacts":

  • We request permission to read your contacts list.
  • We read contact names on-device only to match against Twxn usernames.
  • No contact data is ever uploaded to our servers or stored. Only the Twxn user IDs of confirmed matches are held in memory for the session.
  • You can revoke this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Contacts.

1.6 Push Notifications (Optional)

If you enable push notifications, your APNs device token is stored in your account to deliver price drop alerts and social activity notifications. You can disable these at any time in Settings → Notifications → Twxn.

1.7 Analytics

We use PostHog for analytics. PostHog receives anonymous interaction events — your user ID (not name or email), app version, session duration, swipe counts, and retailer tap counts. It does not receive the content of chat messages or your beauty profile details. Refer to PostHog's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account exists.
  • Swipe history and ratings are retained to power your recommendations. Deleting your account removes all data.
  • Chat history can be deleted within the app at any time.
  • Analytics data is retained by PostHog per their policy (typically 1–2 years).
  • Groq processes chat data transiently — refer to Groq's policy for specifics.

5. Data Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS.
  • Data at rest is stored in Supabase with row-level security — your data is only accessible by your account.
  • Our AI integration uses Supabase Edge Functions so your Groq API key is never exposed to client devices.

6. Children's Privacy

Twxn is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.
